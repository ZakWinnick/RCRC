Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Redwood Coast Rivian Club is a community of Rivian enthusiasts in Northern California. We're not a corporation, and we don't have a complicated data operation. This policy explains, in plain language, what information our website collects and how it's used.

What We Collect

Newsletter Signups (MailerLite)

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address through MailerLite, our email marketing service. That's it. We use your email to send you trail reports, event announcements, and community updates. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email.

Hosting Logs (GitHub Pages)

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub may collect basic server logs when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. We don't have direct access to these logs. You can read GitHub's privacy statement for details.

Social Embeds (Instagram / Meta)

Our site may include embedded content from Instagram. When these embeds load, Instagram (Meta) may collect data about your visit, including cookies. This happens through Meta's embed scripts, not through us. See Instagram's privacy policy for more information.

How We Use Your Data

  • Newsletter delivery: Sending you the updates you signed up for.
  • Community engagement: Sharing events, group drives, and club news.

We don't sell your data. We don't run ads. We don't do analytics tracking. We're a car club, not an ad network.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses a few third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • MailerLite — Email newsletter delivery. Privacy policy
  • Instagram / Meta — Social media embeds. Privacy policy
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting. Privacy statement
  • Google Fonts — Typography. Privacy policy
  • Font Awesome (cdnjs) — Icons on the main site. Cloudflare privacy policy

Cookies

Our site itself doesn't set cookies. However, third-party services embedded on our pages (like Instagram embeds and Google Fonts) may set their own cookies. These are governed by those services' own cookie policies.

If you'd like to control cookies, you can adjust your browser's cookie settings or use a browser extension to block third-party cookies.

Your Choices

  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter anytime via the link in any email.
  • Block cookies through your browser settings.
  • Disable JavaScript to prevent social media embeds from loading (the site still works without JS).

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out at info@redwoodcoastrivianclub.com.
